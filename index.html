Your whole portfolio, valued automatically

Investanco tracks what you own — across institutions and asset classes — and values it every day from public market data. No broker login, no scraping. You enter your holdings once; prices, FX and indices update themselves.

Know exactly where your money stands

Holdings, valuation, allocation and history — computed for you and synced across web and Android.

📒

Holdings & records

Institutions, assets and transactions — buy, sell, dividend — one at a time or via bulk CSV import with a review screen.

📈

Automatic valuation

Per-holding market value, profit/loss and return, updated daily — including fixed-income accrual (CDI, Selic, prefixed, IPCA+).

🎯

Allocation & rebalancing

Set a target percentage per class, see real-vs-target at a glance, and get a rebalancing plan to hit your goals.

🌐

Multi-asset coverage

BR equities, FIIs, ETFs and BDRs, US equities, crypto and Tesouro Direto — valued from public market data in BRL and USD.

📅

Daily snapshots

Daily snapshots of total value, invested and profit/loss build a history you can chart over time.

☁️

Cloud sync

Firebase Auth (Google) with Firestore as the source of truth and a single-owner lock. A local cache renders instantly and refreshes in the background.

Automatic, from public data

You enter holdings. Investanco does the math.

The only manual data is what you own — and that changes rarely. Everything that moves daily (prices, FX, indices) is fetched automatically from public sources. No broker credentials, no scraping, no aggregator.
